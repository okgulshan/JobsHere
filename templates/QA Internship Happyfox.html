QA Internship - Happyfox

Job Title : Quality Assurance Intern
Company : Happyfox
Location : Bangalore
Job Type : Internship (3 months)

Job Description
HappyFox is delighted to offer an exciting opportunity for a Quality Assurance Intern to join our team for a 3-month internship. As a Quality Assurance Intern, you will play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and reliability of our software products. This internship provides hands-on experience in a vibrant and innovative work environment.
Responsibilities
  1. Testing and Quality Assurance:
    • Conduct thorough testing of HappyFox software products to identify and report defects.
    • Collaborate with the development team to understand product features and functionalities.
  2. Test Plan Development:
    • Assist in the creation and execution of comprehensive test plans.
    • Document and communicate test results and issues effectively.
  3. Automation:
    • Explore opportunities for test automation and contribute to the development of automated test scripts.
    • Gain exposure to industry-standard QA tools and methodologies.
  4. Collaboration:
    • Work closely with cross-functional teams, including developers and product managers.
    • Participate in team meetings and contribute to discussions on product quality.
  5. Learning and Development:
    • Gain hands-on experience with HappyFox products and technologies.
    • Participate in training sessions and workshops to enhance your technical and professional skills.
Gains and Benefits
  • Certificates: Receive a certificate of completion at the end of the internship, recognizing your contributions and the skills gained during the program.
  • Letter of Recommendation: Obtain a personalized letter of recommendation based on your performance during the internship.
  • Performance-based Stipend: Enjoy a stipend of up to 7,000 rupees, with the potential for additional incentives based on exceptional performance during the 3-month internship period.
  • Skill Enhancement: Improve your testing and quality assurance skills, and gain exposure to test automation and industry best practices.
Qualifications
  • Currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field.
  • Strong interest in software testing, quality assurance, and attention to detail.
  • Basic understanding of software development and testing concepts is a plus.
  • Familiarity with testing tools and methodologies is an advantage.
  • Excellent communication and teamwork skills.
  • Eagerness to learn and contribute to a dynamic work environment.
Additional Information
  • This is a 3-month internship position with the possibility of extension based on performance and business needs.
  • The internship offers valuable insights into the software industry and an opportunity to work with experienced professionals at HappyFox.

If you are a dedicated student looking for a rewarding internship experience in quality assurance with the opportunity to earn a stipend of up to 7,000 rupees, HappyFox invites you to apply. Join us for a dynamic learning experience in the world of software quality. Apply now!
