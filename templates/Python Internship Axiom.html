Python Internship - Axiom Corporation

Job Title : Python Intern
Company : Axiom Corporation
Location : Pune
Job Type : Internship (2 months)

Job Description
Axiom Corporation is thrilled to offer an enriching opportunity for a Python Intern to join our team for a 2-month internship. As a Python Intern, you will be immersed in real-world projects, gaining hands-on experience in Python development within a dynamic corporate setting.
Responsibilities
  1. Python Development:
    • Contribute to Python programming projects under the guidance of experienced developers.
    • Work on tasks related to software development, debugging, and optimization.
  2. Collaboration:
    • Engage in collaborative coding sessions with the development team.
    • Participate in team meetings to discuss project requirements and progress.
  3. Learning and Skill Development:
    • Gain practical experience with Python and related technologies.
    • Expand your knowledge of software development practices and industry standards.
  4. Problem Solving:
    • Tackle coding challenges and problem-solving tasks to enhance your programming skills.
    • Work on real-world scenarios to apply your Python knowledge.
Gains and Benefits
  • Certificates: Receive a certificate of completion at the end of the internship, recognizing your contributions and the skills gained during the program.
  • Letter of Recommendation: Obtain a personalized letter of recommendation based on your performance during the internship.
  • Performance-based Stipend: Enjoy a stipend of up to 4,000 rupees, with the potential for additional incentives based on exceptional performance during the 2-month internship period.
  • Real-world Python Experience: Work on actual projects, applying Python programming concepts in a corporate environment.
Qualifications
  • Currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field.
  • Strong interest and foundational knowledge in Python programming.
  • Familiarity with software development concepts and basic coding practices.
  • Excellent problem-solving and communication skills.
  • Eagerness to learn and contribute to a fast-paced development environment.
Additional Information
  • This is a 2-month internship position with the possibility of extension based on performance and business needs.
  • The internship offers valuable insights into Python development and an opportunity to work with experienced professionals at Axiom Corporation.

If you are a motivated student eager to gain practical experience in Python development, Axiom Corporation invites you to apply. Join us for a dynamic learning experience and elevate your Python programming skills. Apply now!
