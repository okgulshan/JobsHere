Sales Manager - L'Oréal

Job Title : Sales Manager
Company : L'Oréal
Location : New Delhi
Job Type : Full-time.

Job Description
L'Oréal, the world leader in beauty and cosmetics, is seeking a dynamic and results-driven Sales Manager to join our dedicated team. As a Sales Manager, you will play a key role in driving revenue growth, managing a high-performing sales team, and fostering strong relationships with key clients. The ideal candidate will have a proven track record in sales leadership, a deep understanding of the beauty and cosmetics industry, and a passion for achieving ambitious sales targets.
Responsibilities
  1. Sales Leadership:
    • Develop and implement strategic sales plans to achieve and exceed sales targets.
    • Lead, motivate, and manage a team of sales professionals to deliver exceptional performance.
    • Provide guidance and support to the sales team, fostering a culture of continuous improvement and excellence.
  2. Market Analysis:
    • Conduct thorough market research to identify trends, opportunities, and challenges in the beauty and cosmetics industry.
    • Utilize market insights to develop and adjust sales strategies for maximum effectiveness.
  3. Client Relationship Management:
    • Build and maintain strong relationships with key clients, understanding their business needs and ensuring client satisfaction.
    • Collaborate with clients to identify new business opportunities and drive long-term partnerships.
  4. Team Development:
    • Recruit, train, and develop the sales team to enhance their skills and capabilities.
    • Conduct regular performance evaluations and provide constructive feedback to foster professional growth.
  5. Budget Management:
    • Develop and manage the sales budget, ensuring optimal resource allocation for maximum return on investment.
    • Monitor and analyze sales performance against budget, implementing corrective actions as needed.
  6. Collaboration:
    • Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including marketing, product development, and supply chain, to ensure alignment and success in achieving overall business objectives.
Qualifications
  • Bachelor's degree in Business, Marketing, or a related field (MBA preferred).
  • Proven experience as a Sales Manager in the beauty and cosmetics industry.
  • Strong understanding of beauty and cosmetic products, market trends, and consumer behavior.
  • Exceptional leadership, communication, and interpersonal skills.
  • Results-driven with a proven track record of meeting and exceeding sales targets.
  • Ability to analyze data, draw insights, and make data-driven decisions.
  • Demonstrated ability to build and maintain strong client relationships.
  • Excellent negotiation and presentation skills.

If you are a strategic thinker, a natural leader, and have a passion for the beauty industry, we invite you to join our team at L'Oréal. Apply now and be part of a company that is dedicated to making the world more beautiful every day.
