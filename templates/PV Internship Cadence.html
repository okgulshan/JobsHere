PV Internship - Cadence

Job Title : Product Validation Intern
Company : Cadence Design Systems
Location : Noida
Job Type : Internship (2 months)

Job Description
Cadence Design Systems is offering an exciting opportunity for a Product Validation Intern to join our team for a 2-month internship. As a Product Validation Intern, you will have the chance to work on cutting-edge technologies in the field of electronic design automation (EDA) and contribute to the validation and testing of our industry-leading software products. This internship provides a hands-on experience in a dynamic and innovative environment.
Responsibilities
  1. Testing and Validation:
    • Conduct validation testing on Cadence software products to ensure they meet quality standards.
    • Work closely with the product development team to understand product features and functionalities.
  2. Test Plan Development:
    • Collaborate with senior validation engineers to develop and execute test plans.
    • Document and report issues, defects, and test results.
  3. Automation:
    • Explore opportunities for test automation and contribute to the development of automated test scripts.
    • Gain exposure to industry-standard validation tools and methodologies.
  4. Collaboration:
    • Work closely with cross-functional teams, including developers and quality assurance engineers.
    • Participate in team meetings and contribute ideas to improve product quality.
  5. Learning and Development:
    • Take advantage of the learning opportunities provided during the internship.
    • Gain hands-on experience with Cadence tools and technologies.
    • Participate in training sessions and workshops to enhance technical skills.
Gains and Benefits
  • Real-world Experience: Get hands-on experience working on actual projects and contributing to the validation of industry-leading software used in electronic design.
  • Skill Enhancement: Improve your testing and validation skills, and gain exposure to test automation, scripting languages, and industry best practices.
  • Certificates: Receive a certificate of completion at the end of the internship, recognizing your contributions and the skills gained during the program.
  • Performance-based Stipend: Enjoy a stipend of up to 5 rupees, with the potential for additional incentives based on exceptional performance during the 2-month internship period.
Qualifications
  • Currently pursuing a degree in Electrical Engineering, Computer Science, or a related field.
  • Strong interest in software testing, validation, and quality assurance.
  • Basic understanding of electronic design automation concepts is a plus.
  • Programming/scripting skills (e.g., Python, Perl, or Tcl) is an advantage.
  • Excellent communication and teamwork skills.
  • Eagerness to learn and contribute to a fast-paced environment.
Additional Information
  • This is a 2-month internship position with the possibility of extension based on performance and business needs.
  • The internship offers valuable insights into the EDA industry and an opportunity to work with experienced professionals.
  • Previous experience with large-scale distributed systems.

If you are a motivated student looking for a short-term internship to gain hands-on experience in product validation within the EDA industry, Cadence Design Systems invites you to apply. Join us for a dynamic learning experience in the heart of innovation. Apply now!
